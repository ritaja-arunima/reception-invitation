About Ritaja

Ritaja currently fuels the engineering team at Adnymics Munich. His work revolves around designing efficient software that predicts the likes and dislikes of customers to produce intelligent targeted advertisements. His love for artificial intelligence was sculpted into research skils for the industry at the Technical Universiy of Munich (TUM), where he persued his masters in Computer Science.

About Arunima

Arunima is currently working at the Comprehensive Pneumology Centre, Helmholtz Zentrum as a PhD research-fellow. Her work is focussed on the novel extra-cellular matrix proteins in lung fibrosis. Her interest towards the field of Lung biology was strengthened at the Ludwig Maximilian University (LMU), Munich, where she persued her Masters.
Arunima is indoctrinated in classical vocal singing and when she is neither deeply involved in research nor polishing her skills, she enjoys hiking and travelling.
